    ABOUT ME

    Passion Meets Precision

    I am Nguyen Ba Tuong — a 3D Artist dedicated to creating high-quality, production-ready assets. With a deep focus on clean topology and PBR texturing, I transform concepts into optimized 3D models that seamlessly integrate into games, films, and real-time environments.

    I love the art of digital craftsmanship, ensuring every polygon and material texture tells a story. My goal is to deliver technical precision and visual excellence for every asset I build.
